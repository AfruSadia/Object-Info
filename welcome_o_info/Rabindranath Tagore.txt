Rabindranath Tagore FRAS, sobriquet Gurudev, was a Bengali polymath from the Indian subcontinent, a poet, musician and artist. He reshaped Bengali literature and music, as well as Indian art with Contextual Modernism in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. 
Born: May 7, 1861, Kolkata, India
Died: August 7, 1941, Kolkata, India
Movies: Charulata, Teen Kanya, Ghare-Baire, Chokher Bali.
