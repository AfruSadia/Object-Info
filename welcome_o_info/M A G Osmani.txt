Muhammad Ataul Gani Osmani, also known as Bangabir, was the commander-in-chief of the Bangladesh Forces during the 1971 Bangladesh War of Independence. Osmani's career spanned five decades, beginning with service in the British Indian Army in 1939. 
Born: September 1, 1918, Sunamganj District
Died: February 16, 1984, London, United Kingdom
Education: Aligarh Muslim University
Other name: Bangabir 
Place of burial: Sylhet
Battles and wars: World War II, Bangladesh Liberation War
