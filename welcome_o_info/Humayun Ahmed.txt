Bangladeshi writer
DescriptionHumayun Ahmed was a Bangladeshi writer, dramatist, screenwriter, filmmaker, songwriter, scholar, and lecturer. His breakthrough was his debut novel Nondito Noroke published in 1972. He wrote over 200 fiction and non-fiction books, many of which were bestsellers in Bangladesh. 
Born: November 13, 1948, Netrokona District
Died: July 19, 2012, Bellevue Hospital Center, New York City, New York, United States
Movies: Debi, Srabon Megher Din, Amar Ache Jol, MORE
Spouse: Meher Afroz Shaon (m. 2005–2012), Gultekin Ahmed (m. 1976–2003)
Siblings: Muhammed Zafar Iqbal, Ahsan Habib, Sufia Haider, Momtaz Shahid, Rukhsana Ahmed
