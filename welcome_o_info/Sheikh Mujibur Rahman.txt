Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, shortened as Sheikh Mujib or just Mujib, was a Bangladeshi politician and statesman. He is the founding father of the People's Republic of Bangladesh. He is known as "Jatir Pita" or "Bongobondhu" of Bangladesh.
Born: March 17, 1920, Tungipara Upazila
Height: 1.8 m
Assassinated: August 15, 1975, Dhaka
Education: Maulana Azad College, University of Dhaka, University of Calcutta
Parents: Sayera Khatun, Sheikh Lutfur Rahman
