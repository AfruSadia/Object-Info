Muhammed Zafar Iqbal is a Bangladeshi science fiction author, physicist, academic and activist. He is a professor of computer science and engineering at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology. As of January 2018, he is the head of Electrical and Electronic Engineering department. 
Born: December 23, 1952 (age 65 years), Sylhet
Spouse: Yasmeen Haque (m. 1978)
Movies: Amar Bondhu Rashed, Ankhi O Tar Bandhura
Siblings: Humayun Ahmed, Ahsan Habib, Sufia Haider, Momtaz Shahid, Rukhsana Ahmed
Children: Yeshim Iqbal
